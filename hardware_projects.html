Hardware Projects

YCP Hacks 2024

For the 2024 YCP Hacks event, me and my team used a Raspberry PI device to create a man-in-the-middle USB device. This device, when plugged in, would automatically send keystrokes to the victim computer, and execute a series of of shell commands on the computer, allowing us to scrape files from the victim machine!

We wrote up and planned our presentation, and demonstrated our project in front of a panel of judges and other hackers. We ended up winning the "Best Hardware Hack" award for our project!
